Roles and Permissions

Roles and privleges allow you to control permission across your business. Create and manage roles and then assign them to your admins.

DESCRIPTION

Owner
	
Has all Administration, People Management, Payments, and Finance permissions, and can delete account
	


Super Admin
	
Has all Administration, People Management, Payments, and Finance permissions
	


Admin
	
Has most permissions, but cannot view or edit company settings

Has all People Management, Payments, and some Finance permissions
	


Manager

Finance
	
Has all Finance and most Payments permissions
